Who We Are
A holding company built on precision

Kopperstone is a Canadian holding company headquartered in Toronto, operating at the intersection of luxury design and high-performance construction across North America and Latin America.

We were founded on a single conviction: that premium materials and expert execution should be accessible to every ambitious project, regardless of geography. We act as the bridge between the world's finest suppliers and the clients who demand the very best.

Through two specialist subsidiaries — Kopperstone Design and Kopperstone Construction — we deliver end-to-end solutions for the most demanding residential and commercial projects on the continent.
