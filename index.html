About Kopperstone

Kopperstone is a Canadian trade company focused on building commercial bridges between suppliers and buyers. Our objective is to support efficient sourcing, dependable communication, and practical deal flow in international markets.

What we stand for

  • Professional business representation across markets
  • Clear coordination between clients and suppliers
  • Long-term relationships built on trust and responsiveness
  • Opportunities in construction, materials, and industrial supply
